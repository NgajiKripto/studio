# **App Name**: Muakeup

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Browsing: Users can view a comprehensive catalog of makeup products, sorted by various attributes like brand and category.
- Criterion-Based Product Filtering: Enable users to filter makeup products based on specific criteria such as skin type, skin tone, and face shape, utilizing the detailed product tagging in the database.
- AI-Powered Personalized Recommendations: An AI tool that suggests relevant products to users based on their selected personal criteria (skin type, skin tone, face shape) by leveraging product attributes and MUA Verdicts for intelligent matching.
- Detailed Product Information: Display rich product details including name, brand, description, estimated price, high-quality images, and a direct affiliate purchase URL.
- MUA Verdicts Display: Showcase expert insights and specific recommendations from the professional makeup artist (MUA) for each product.
- Product Data Management: A backend interface (API) to allow the MUA owner to securely add, edit, and manage product listings along with their associated skin type, skin tone, and face shape criteria in the PostgreSQL database.
- SEO-Optimized Product Listings: Ensure product detail pages are technically optimized for search engines with dynamic meta tags and structured data to enhance discoverability and ranking.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Scheme: A refined, light aesthetic reflecting elegance and professionalism, suitable for a beauty and makeup platform. Primary Color: A deep, sophisticated plum (#844594) evoking luxury and depth. Background Color: A soft, barely-there lilac-gray (#F7F0F8) providing a clean and inviting canvas. Accent Color: A vibrant, analogous lavender-violet (#A370EB) for calls to action and highlighting key features, ensuring visual pop and good contrast.
- Headlines: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif) for its personality, elegant flow, and alignment with fashion and design. Body Text: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) to complement the elegance of 'Belleza' while providing excellent readability for product descriptions and MUA verdicts, offering an intellectual and contemporary feel.
- Utilize minimalist, delicate icons with clean lines that are contextually relevant to beauty, makeup, and personalized filtering options, maintaining a sophisticated and modern feel.
- Employ a mobile-first, responsive grid layout for optimal viewing across all devices. Focus on a clean, product-centric design with ample whitespace, easy navigation, and intuitive filtering options.
- Incorporate subtle, fluid transitions for product filtering, category browsing, and content loading. Animations should feel smooth and premium, enhancing the user experience without being distracting.
